    Meet the Hajster

    Hajster is an innovative leader in the field of heat pumps, dedicated to providing advanced and environmentally conscious solutions for the Ukrainian market.

    Founded in 2000 and was one of the first to offer advanced European and global energy-efficient technologies based on renewable energy sources for the Ukrainian market.

    Today, the company has 23 years of experience in the design and installation of climate systems, and all this potential has been invested in the development of the innovative Ukrainian heat pump - Hajster.

    The development and production of Hajster heat pumps use the best global components and are 100% located in Ukraine.
